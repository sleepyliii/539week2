Stew Eggs

A comforting meal, suitable for any time of the day, is steamed egg with minced pork. The velvety egg curd is complemented by crispy minced pork in a savory sauce. You can prepare this delightful one-bowl dish in under 30 minutes, and it can be adapted to be gluten-free.

Whisked with the perfect amount of water, the eggs are steamed to create an incredibly tender, silky, and smooth curd that simply melts in your mouth. Meanwhile, the minced pork is stir-fried to a crispy perfection and then simmered in a straightforward savory sauce with a touch of sweetness. The interplay of contrasting textures and flavors truly highlights the brilliance of these uncomplicated ingredients!
